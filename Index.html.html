Projects

Selected projects in robotics, drones and energy.

Below are some projects that illustrate my work on agricultural drones, aerial robotics research and renewable energy systems.

25 kg agricultural drone platform

Lead development of a 25 kg payload agricultural drone using Indian technologies, in collaboration with Urban Matrix in India and the French team at ATV. Managed the project, coordinated communication between teams and travelled to India to learn and validate the fabrication process.

Focus: drone design, project management, international collaboration, testing and validation for field use.

Bio-inspired passive legs for drones

Research internship at CNRS on passive leg models for aerial drones inspired by avian biomechanics. Analyzed equilibrium and stability of different leg designs, built mathematical models and validated them with MATLAB simulations to improve landing stability and energy absorption.

Keywords: aerial robotics, biomechanics, MATLAB, modeling and stability analysis.

Vertical axis wind turbine optimization

Academic project on optimizing the performance of a vertical axis wind turbine (VLAWT). Explored blade geometries, simulated power output and studied the effect of magnetic levitation on the turbine axis to reduce friction and wear.

Used Creo Parametric for 3D modeling, CAD and simulation of key components.

Tethered drone and ground robot system

Ongoing project combining a tethered drone with a ground robot to extend flight time and improve safety. Using ROS on the ground robot and Pixhawk on the drone with Raspberry Pi 5, integrating LiDAR and cameras, and working on trajectory planning, SLAM and tether management.

Topics: ROS, Pixhawk, SLAM, tethered drone systems and multi-robot collaboration.

Fanuc & Borunte robot integration

Installation and programming of Fanuc robots with custom-designed grippers, from prototype testing to validated production cells. Reused the same Fanuc system across multiple products and projects, simplifying tooling and solving the complexity of multi-function robots working in the same environment.

Also includes work with 6-axis Borunte robots for pick-and-place of industrial parts, optimizing trajectories and cycle time.
